1.image
wurstmeister/zookeeper

wurstmeister/kafka

2.設定kafka環境變數

KAFKA_ZOOKEEPER_CONNECT terryzookeeper.eastus.azurecontainer.io:2181
KAFKA_LISTENERS PLAINTEXT://:9092
KAFKA_ADVERTISED_LISTENERS PLAINTEXT://terrykafka.eastus.azurecontainer.io:9092
KAFKA_ADVERTISED_HOST_NAME terrykafka.eastus.azurecontainer.io
KAFKA_AUTO_CREATE_TOPICS_ENABLE false
KAFKA_DELETE_TOPIC_ENABLE true

CMD
/opt/kafka_2.13-2.6.0/bin/kafka-topics.sh --create --zookeeper terryzookeeper.eastus.azurecontainer.io:2181 --replication-factor 1 --partitions 1 --topic testTopic
/opt/kafka_2.13-2.6.0/bin/kafka-topics.sh --list --zookeeper terryzookeeper.eastus.azurecontainer.io:2181

/opt/kafka_2.13-2.6.0/bin/kafka-console-producer.sh --broker-list terrykafka.eastus.azurecontainer.io:9092 --topic testTopic
/opt/kafka_2.13-2.6.0/bin/kafka-console-consumer.sh --bootstrap-server terrykafka.eastus.azurecontainer.io:9092 --topic testTopic --from-beginning

/opt/kafka_2.13-2.6.0/bin/kafka-topics.sh --delete --zookeeper terryzookeeper.eastus.azurecontainer.io:2181 --topic testTopic


如果要重啟或刪除Topic 
可以直接刪除zookeeper容器再重新架設
在重啟kafka
